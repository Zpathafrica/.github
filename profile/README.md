# ZPath

Financial privacy for people who need it most.

ZPath is a WhatsApp guide that takes anyone from zero knowledge to their first private Zcash transaction. No app to install. No account to create. Just a conversation — in English and Kiswahili.

---

## What we do

Most people who need financial privacy have never heard of Zcash. Every mobile money transaction they make is recorded, stored, and accessible to operators, authorities, and data partners. Zcash shielded transactions are mathematically private — verified on the blockchain, invisible to everyone else.

ZPath closes the gap between the technology and the people it was built for.

We start with East Africa because the need is greatest here. A market trader in Nairobi, a journalist in Kampala, a family receiving money from the diaspora — these are the people ZPath is built for.

---

## How it works

Anyone can start by sending a message to the ZPath WhatsApp number.

The bot guides them through three layers. First, understanding what Zcash is and why privacy matters. Second, getting their first ZEC — with step by step guidance for buying with M-Pesa, or practicing free on testnet. Third, making a real shielded transaction and verifying it on-chain.

The whole process takes about 45 minutes. It works on any phone. It remembers where you left off.

---

## Built on Zcash

ZPath uses Zcash's shielded transaction system — the most advanced cryptographic privacy in production cryptocurrency today. When a user completes onboarding, their transaction is confirmed on the Zcash blockchain. The verification is real.

ZPath does not hold funds. Does not custody assets. Does not store phone numbers in readable form. The product is a guide, not a platform.
